Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the Time.Cards service that was created by Dasix, Inc. This policy describes the types of personal information we may collect and how we use it to provide the Time.Cards service. By using Time.Cards, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy. We collect limited account information on our secure servers. We will never rent, sell or share information that personally identifies you for marketing purposes with your permission.

Information We Collect

When you register for Time.Cards, we collect the following types of personal and sensitive user data:

  • We collect your phone number for authentication purposes. This allows us to verify your identity and provide secure access to our app.
  • After the initial authentication process, we ask for your first name and last name to personalize your experience within the app.
  • You have the option to provide an email address for support purposes. We may use this email address to communicate with you regarding support inquiries, such as responding to chats or opening support tickets.

How We Use Your Information

When you register for Time.Cards, we collect the following types of personal and sensitive user data:

  • Authentication: Your phone number is used for authentication purposes to provide secure access to our app.
  • Personalization: Your first name and last name are used to personalize your experience within the app.
  • Support: If you provide an email address, we may use it to communicate with you regarding support inquiries or to provide assistance.

Sharing of Information

We share your personal and sensitive user data with the following third-party service providers for the purposes described below:

  • Auth0: We share your phone number with Auth0 for authentication purposes.
  • Twilio: We share your phone number with Twilio for sending the one-time password via text message.
  • UserEcho: If you provide an email address, it will be stored and processed by UserEcho for support purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of your personal and sensitive user data. This includes encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your information.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal and sensitive user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. If you wish to request the deletion of your data, please contact us at support@dasix.com.

Children

We do not knowing collect personal information from children under 16, or equivalent minimum age in relevant jurisdiction, unless their parent provided verifiable consent. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, or equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction, without consent of their parent, we will take steps to delete this information as soon as possible. Parents or guardians can contact us.

Third-Party Sites and Services

Our website or any of our services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the information collected by those third parties, and we encourage you to read their privacy policy before submitting any personal information to them.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. This policy is effective as of 2024-01-01.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@dasix.com.
